## Politicians

Making decisions is hard and takes time. How many pairs of shoes do you try on before you decide which pair to buy? And you are the only person involved in making that decision. How would you decide on bigger issues that would impact on more that your comfort (and anyone who might suffer the consequences of you your discomfort if the shoes are ill-fitting). How much information would you need? What principles would you use to guide the decision? How will the decision be recieved by the other people it affects? How do you gather and synthesise all that information and do your job and care for you family? Unless doing it is your job, in which case you are a politican.

So, when you have a complex decision to make that will require a study to understand and will effect a large number of people who will have their own opinion on what the right decision should be the best solution is to find a politician who agrees (as close as possible) the decision you would make and given them the task of agreeing with the other parties so that the dicision can be taken and implemented. Its a great solution as you don't have to compromise on what you believe is the right decision and you have someone else to blame for the compromises that they needed to make to reconcile the different positions of the parties involved in making the decision. Which ultimately means that politicians by the nature of their work in negotiating compromises so that they can get things done will betray their constituents. Remember that action needs to be taken and compromise is necessary.

### What is a politician and why do they matter?

Politicians are individuals who are elected or appointed to represent the interests of their constituents or constituents in a government or political party. They play a vital role in shaping public policy, representing their constituents' interests, and promoting the common good. Politicians are responsible for making decisions that affect the lives of millions of people, and their actions can have far-reaching consequences.
